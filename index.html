Who We Are

Privately operated from Centerville, Tennessee, with fulfillment capacity that scales when demand spikes.

Founded in 2025, Charmers began with a simple promise: make meaningful accessories that people actually want to wear. We now operate structured functions across product design, production, logistics, customer support, and quality assurance. Our processes prioritize clarity, speed, and reliability, supported by documentation and ongoing staff training.

Logistics note: Our internal Charmers Logistics Union (CLU) coordinates shipments and hand-offs. CLU primarily leverages USPS services with limited exceptions for specific local or employee deliveries.

Technology & Security

Payments through Stripe, with additional checks to protect customers and our staff.

Our checkout flows are designed around reliability and transparency. We support mainstream payment methods via Stripe, including card payments and compatible wallets where available. Sensitive card data is handled by Stripe; Charmers does not store card numbers on our servers. We log key order metadata—like contact information and shipping details—to help you track and receive your items.

Our public sites are hosted across reputable providers and protected with human verification on sensitive forms as needed. We continuously audit frontend logic for total calculation accuracy and display helpful error messages when payment attempts fail.

Logistics: CLU

Charmers Logistics Union orchestrates packing, labeling, and carrier hand‑offs.

Processing Windows

Standard orders are processed quickly, with options to prioritize preparation during peak seasons. You’ll receive confirmation once your order is packed and handed off.

Labeling & Tracking

Labels include machine-readable barcodes and QR codes to accelerate sortation. You’ll see major tracking milestones after carrier acceptance and while in transit.

Packaging Standards

Padded mailers and reinforced envelopes are matched to bracelet size. We avoid unnecessary bulk to keep costs low while protecting your items in transit.

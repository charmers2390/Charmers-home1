1) Who We Are & Scope

Charmers designs, sells, and ships bracelets and related accessories, and operates the Charmers Logistics Union (“CLU”) to coordinate first‑mile handling and handoff to national carriers. We also operate AI/assistive services under the “LunaBot AI” brand. This Policy applies to your interactions with our Covered Services above. It does not apply to third‑party websites or services that we do not control.

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We share information only as described in this Policy, such as with service providers (e.g., payment processors, security vendors, logistics partners) to operate our services.

2) Definitions

  • Personal Information (PI): Information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be linked with an identifiable individual.
  • Sensitive PI: Certain categories of PI (e.g., precise geolocation, financial account access credentials) granted special protection under applicable laws.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on data (collection, storage, use, sharing, deletion).
  • Controller / Business: The entity that decides why and how PI is processed (here, Charmers).
  • Processor / Service Provider: An entity that processes PI on behalf of the Controller/Business (e.g., payment processing).

3) Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Identity & Contact: name, email, phone, shipping address, country.
  • Order & Customization: bracelet text, size, style selections, gift messages.
  • Support & Communications: messages sent via email, SMS, contact forms, or phone recordings as permitted by law.
  • Employment/Contracting: job application data submitted on hires.charmersbiz.org (e.g., resume details) after human verification checks.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device & Usage: IP address, browser, OS, device identifiers, referral URLs, pages viewed, time on site, and actions performed.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: preferences, session tokens, cart contents, fraud signals. See Cookies.
  • Anti‑abuse telemetry: signals from security tools (e.g., bot detection, human verification) to protect our sites and users.

3.3 Information from third parties

  • Payments: from payment processors (e.g., Stripe) regarding transaction status; we do not receive full card numbers or CVCs.
  • Logistics: from carriers (e.g., USPS) and CLU systems for shipment status and delivery events.
  • Fraud & Security: from threat‑intel and verification services (e.g., CAPTCHA/Turnstile).

4) Sources of Information

We collect PI directly from you when you place an order or contact us; automatically via your use of our sites; and from service providers who help us complete payments, fulfill orders, and maintain security.

8) Payments (Stripe, Wallets)

Payments are processed by Stripe. Depending on your device, you may see options like card input, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Link, or Cash App Pay. When you pay, certain PI is exchanged with Stripe to create a charge and screen for fraud. Charmers does not receive full card numbers or CVCs. We do receive payment confirmations, tokens, and limited metadata (e.g., last4, brand, expiration month/year) to reconcile orders and provide support.

If you use wallet payments, we receive confirmation of success/failure and basic details needed to fulfill your order. When a payment fails, we receive error codes or messages sufficient to help you retry securely.

9) Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, localStorage, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, maintain your cart, and protect against fraud. You can control cookies in your browser. Blocking cookies may impact site functionality (e.g., checkout, tracking pages).

  • Strictly necessary: security tokens, session identifiers, cart persistence.
  • Functional: preferences such as default shipping options.
  • Performance: limited analytics to improve reliability and speed.
  • Security/Verification: human verification and anti‑abuse tokens.

We use minimal analytics and multiple security measures to help protect the integrity of our services. These systems may observe IP address, device characteristics, request rates, and interaction patterns to distinguish legitimate activity from automated or abusive behavior. Human verification challenges may be presented when needed. We also record limited logs to diagnose issues and prevent misuse.

Annex C — CLU Logistics & Tracking

What CLU Is: The Charmers Logistics Union handles first‑mile preparation, QC scans, and routing to national carriers. CLU tracking pages may show internal steps prior to USPS acceptance. Once handed off, USPS or the receiving carrier continues updates.

Data Used: package ID, scan events, facility codes, timestamps, driver or station identifiers (as internal codes), and delivery confirmations. CLU uses this data to display status, troubleshoot delivery issues, and maintain operational metrics. CLU does not publish driver PII.

When You Contact Support: We may view your tracking events and address details to assist you. We do not publicly expose your phone or email on tracking pages.

Annex D — Stripe Data Flow (Narrative)

  1. You enter payment details on a Stripe Elements form or initiate a supported wallet.
  2. Your device communicates directly with Stripe to tokenize payment info.
  3. Charmers receives a token/intent ID from Stripe (not the full card number) and creates a charge.
  4. Stripe screens the transaction for fraud and returns status to Charmers.
  5. Charmers stores order details and Stripe IDs for reconciliation and support; card numbers/CVCs are not stored by Charmers.

Glossary

Service Provider / Processor: A third party that processes PI on our behalf under contractual limits.

Targeted advertising (cross‑context behavioral advertising): Ads based on your activity across non‑affiliated sites; Charmers does not engage in this.
